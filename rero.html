Rero Competition

Each game involves two teams competing at the same time. Before each game begins, a random solution number is generated. Based on the number generated, teams must collect and use numbers spread out on the game field to form equations that equate to the solution number generated.

Each team’s robot must first autonomously navigate the game field via line following to collect numbers represented by cylinders. Based on the numbers collected, participants are given magnets labelled with the said numbers, along with some labelled with mathematical symbols (+, –, ×, ÷). Participants may then form the equations on the whiteboard(s) provided.

The team that gets the most points, accumulated from collecting numbers and forming equations, wins.
